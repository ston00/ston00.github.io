Song Lyric Analysis
(INST 414)

This analysis project was done over the summer. The task we were required to do was to analyze a lyric dataset provided to us then append a song of our choice. With the dataset we created relplot to measure the length of each song by each word according to the year they were released. Each song in this dataset was from the 1900's to early 2000's and what we found out was that as time progressed the length of each song increased as well. Around the early 2000's was when songs had the longest length.

This project allows us to practice the use of scikit learn methods to visualize different fields of data to describe the relationship between them. On another relplot we vectorized the number of unique words to measure how the number of unique words would change as time progressed and we found a similar relation where the number of unique words increased as time progressed. However, this time there was a peak of unique words around the late 1970's and another one around late 1990's. By aggregating and visualizing our data we were able to discover the relationship between time and lyrics of different songs.
