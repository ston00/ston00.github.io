Waze Data Analysis
(Google Advanced Data Analytics Certificate)

I am currently doing a Google Advanced Data Analytics Professional Certificate and throughout this course we are assigned to do capstone projects from different companies such as TikTok, Automatidata, and Waze. The course tasks us to analyze sample datasets from the past years viewing user engagement on the from each company and from there we are able to practice the process of Exploratory Data Analysis by extracting, loading, and transforming data into a central repository. From this course we use libraries like Pandas, NumPy, Seaborn, Matplotlib, etc to translate data into insights.
